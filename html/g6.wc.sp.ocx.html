Lesson 2: What was the air outside like on a day when it hailed?

Navigation

Turn and talk
  1. Imagine you were at a spot where you knew a hailstorm was going to occur. Discuss these questions:
  • What do you think the conditions outside are like on a day when it hails?
  • What data would you want to collect before, during, and after the hail event to try to figure out what caused it?

Hailstone Observations

In your notebook
  1. Make a 2-column chart in your science notebook and record what you notice and wonder about the hailstone images.

Make Sense

With your class
  1. Share with the class:
  • What are some patterns you noticed as you examined the hailstone images?
  • What new ideas and questions do you have about how hailstones are produced?
